About Bahirambaba Temple

Welcome to the holy Bahirambaba Temple at Karanja Bahiram. This is one of the largest and most famous Kuldevata (Family Deity) temples in Maharashtra. Situated at the southern foothills of the Satpura range in the Amravati district, this temple is dedicated to Bhairavnath (a form of Lord Shiva) and serves as the spiritual center for lakhs of devotees across Vidarbha.

ЁЯУЬ History of the Temple

Construction: This temple was built in the early 1800s by a farmer named Tulshiram Madhge. The idol in the temple is Swayambhu (self-manifested), standing about 6 feet tall and 8 to 10 feet wide.

Contribution of Sant Gadge Baba: About 45 years ago, Sant Gadge Maharaj visited here, stopped the practice of animal sacrifice, and promoted rationality in religious practices.
